DNIPRO
BEER MUSEUM

This is a corner for the curious. Here you can find out how exactly beer "flowed" to the city of Dnipro, hear the story of the first breweries and bars of our city.

We will show you exhibits from the soviet period and Katerynoslav. Interactive reconstruction of old factories and live deconstruction of beer in the form of ingredients that you can try to dispel all myths and conjectures about brewing.
After the historical and industrial excursion, you will get to a tasting and consolidate everything you have learned about beer and different varieties.
