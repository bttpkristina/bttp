Wayne Johnson, MD


Dr. Wayne Johnson practices medicine on Hilton Head Island, SC. Through his years of practice he has developed a program to encourage and support people of all ages, and especially young people, in the pursuit of a Joyful Life.

Using techniques of mindfulness this program guides each person through a personal journey to mitigate anxiety, focus on fulfillment, and find their way "Back To The Present."

About

Back to the Present, we believe mindfulness is more than just a practice—it’s a way of living. We help young people recognize their unique physical, emotional, and spiritual gifts, while learning how to manage their time, energy, and attention with purpose.


Through our Mindfulness Mantra, we guide individuals to:


  • Make conscious and intentional choices
  • Understand the difference between needs, wants, and desires
  • Explore emotions, thoughts, and inner strengths
  • Balance productivity, peace, and purpose
  • Approach life with curiosity, compassion, and self-awareness

Our goal is to empower the next generation to live more mindfully, stay present, and find joy in the journey.


Mission Statement

Our mission is to equip young people with the tools of emotional maturity, mindfulness, and self-awareness so they can make wise choices, build meaningful relationships, and live fulfilling lives. Through the Life-Map framework, we aim to introduce these values into education and society, fostering a spiritual revolution that prioritizes personal growth, responsibility, and genuine joy over material success. We believe that by empowering the next generation with emotional intelligence and mindful living, we can create a healthier, happier, and more connected world.


Vision Statement

backtothepresent.org, our vision is to empower young minds to live with presence, purpose, and loving intention.

We imagine a world where adolescents and young adults embrace their Unique Abilities — conscious awareness and free choice — to stay grounded in the present moment rather than being pulled away by distractions. By practicing empathy, communication, sacrifice, and faith, they can nurture meaningful relationships, ease anxiety, and experience a more joyful, connected life.
Our goal is simple: to help young people reclaim their awareness, rise above anxiety, and discover the joy of living fully in the present.
