About

Dr. Wayne Johnson practices medicine on Hilton Head Island, SC. Through his years of practice he has developed a program to encourage and support people of all ages, and especially young people, in the pursuit of a Joyful Life. Using techniques of mindfulness this program guides each person through a personal journey to mitigate anxiety, focus on fulfillment, and find their way "Back To The Present."
